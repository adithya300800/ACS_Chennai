Company Profile

ACS Chennai is a technology-driven Construction Project Management and Business Process Solutions provider, based in Oragadam, Chennai, India. We blend the rigor of traditional infrastructure management with modern digital platforms to deliver robust, scalable, and transparent solutions for the built environment and remote business operations.

Technical Focus

  • Project Management Systems: Cloud-native project management for construction/facility management using Zoho WorkDrive, Zoho Mail, and DevSecOps toolchains.
  • Remote Team Orchestration: Real-time, secure collaboration with cloud storage, document versioning, RBAC, and mobile-first automation.
  • HR & Talent Infrastructure: Automated onboarding, offer letter management, and digital HR compliance.
  • Business Process Automation: APIs and low-code integrations for procurement, payroll, and scheduling workflows.
  • Data Security & Compliance: Encrypted storage, MFA, audit trails, ISO/client compliance.

Current Projects

  • Oragadam Residential Complex: Digital timesheet tracking, biometric attendance, automated vendor invoice processing.
  • Digital HR Stack: Virtual employee onboarding, offer workflow, cloud archival.
  • VirtualOffice 2.0: Multi-tenant platform for SMEs, user provisioning, Zoho WorkDrive, admin dashboards.
  • Mobile Field Operations App: Real-time site updates, work orders, database sync.
